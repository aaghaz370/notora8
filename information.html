Notora — Information & Rules

1. INFORMATION

Notora ek community-powered library platform hai jahan users apne paas mojood books ko share kar sakte hain.
Website ka main focus hai: learning, exploring aur free knowledge access. Koi bhi paid service nahi hai.
Recommended books algorithm: trending searches, most-read books aur community activity ke base par kaam karta hai.
Har user ka data (email/password) hashed form me secure stored hota hai. Password plain-text me kabhi save nahi hota.

2. RULES

Kisi bhi user ko gali dena, beizzat karna ya disturb karna strictly prohibited hai.
Har user apne account aur uploads ka zimmedar khud hoga.
Spam, fake books, malware links ya misleading files upload mat karein.
Repeated bad behaviour se account restrict ya permanently ban ho sakta hai.
Comments me respectful language rakhna mandatory hai.

3. FAQs

Notora free kyu hai?
Ye community-driven platform hai jisme users hi content contribute karte hain. Koi subscription nahi hai.
Kya ye legal hai?
Haan. Users sirf wahi books upload karte hain jo unke paas already mojood hoti hain. Notora kisi bhi copyrighted book ka owner nahi.
Forgot Password system kab aayega?
Feature develop ho raha hai. Tab tak aap password ko kahin note karke rakhein.
Books ka verification kaise hota hai?
Community flags, reports aur moderators ke through suspicious books ko review kiya jata hai.

4. LEGAL

Notora kisi bhi book ka owner ya copyright holder nahi hai. Website sirf users dwara uploaded books ko host karti hai. Hum copyright violation ka support nahi karte. Agar koi user kisi copyrighted content ko upload kare, uska zimma usi user ka hoga. DMCA ya removal request par content 24–48 hours me remove kiya jata hai.
